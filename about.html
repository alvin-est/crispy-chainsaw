About Us

Welcome to Codey, where we bring text to life with the power of speech! Our mission is to provide high-quality text-to-speech services that are accessible, user-friendly, and versatile for a wide range of applications.

Who We Are

At Codey, we are a team of dedicated professionals passionate about the intersection of technology and communication. Our experts come from diverse backgrounds, including software engineering, linguistics, and audio production, all working together to create an exceptional text-to-speech experience.

What We Do

A handy audio model that generate realistic speech and sound effects across multiple available languages. Codey helps to offer a preview into the digital interaction in different contexts. It mainly integrates two server side APIs with responsive voice and google cloud TTS, and to automate the entire conversion process. In minutes, you will have the technology to read the text out loud.

Our Commitment

We are committed to delivering a reliable and efficient text-to-speech service that meets the needs of our users.
